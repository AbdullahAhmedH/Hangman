1)Play individually or in groups.

2)User should select and enter a letter of the alphabet into the given space.

3)If the letter is contained in the word/phrase, the letter will reveal its positions and the group or individual takes another turn guessing a letter.

4)Keep an eye on the number of wrong guesses you can make, as that indicates whether the game continues or not

5)The game continues until:
  (a)The word/phrase is guessed (all letters are revealed) – WINNER or,
  (b)The wrong guess counter reaches 0 – LOSER
